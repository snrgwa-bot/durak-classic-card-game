How to Play

Durak uses a 36-card deck (6 through Ace). The last player holding cards is the "Durak" — the fool and the loser. Here are the basics:

1

The last card drawn from the deck sets the trump suit, which beats all other suits.

2

Attack your opponent by playing a card from your hand onto the table.

3

Defend by placing a higher card of the same suit, or any trump card.

4

If you can't defend, you pick up all table cards and your opponent attacks again.

5

Draw from the deck after each round to refill your hand to six cards.

6

The first player to empty their hand wins. Don't be the Durak!
